Welcome to the Restaurant Sales & Inventory Demo

This demo showcases how Django can serve as a powerful backend platform for building a real-world inventory and sales reporting system—perfect for a small restaurant, food stand, or movie theater.

While the examples shown here are simplified for demonstration purposes, they highlight the core functionality needed to build a robust, scalable system capable of powering real-world operations.

The system tracks:

  • 📦 Ingredient inventory with live updates as items are sold
  • 🍔 Customer orders linked to menu items and associated ingredient usage
  • 📊 Daily and custom date range reports showing revenue, cost, and profit
  • 📈 Interactive visual dashboards powered by Chart.js (e.g. top-selling items, time-of-day sales trends, and profit breakdowns)

Whether you're managing a bustling kitchen or a concessions counter, this prototype illustrates how Django can streamline your operations with data-driven insight and real-time inventory control.
